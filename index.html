The History

 

Accessible for all types of events 1969

Basking Ridge Country Club Catering is open to the community and accessible for all types of events, be it an intimate private dinner party or a lavish celebration with guests in the hundreds. Overlooking 258 private acres with panoramic views of the manicured fairways and beautiful Somerset Hills, our facility is traditional & elegant, featuring a raised hearth fireplaces, mirrored French doors, crystal chandeliers and customizable lighting. Basking Ridge Country Club Catering’s friendly and professional event coordinators will guide you through every step of your function. It is our passion to provide warm hospitality, excellent service, and superior quality, creating a memorable experience for you and your guests
